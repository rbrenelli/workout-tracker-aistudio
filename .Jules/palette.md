## 2024-05-24 - Provide Contextual Recovery Actions for Empty States
**Learning:** Empty search states without recovery actions create friction, leading to a confusing user experience. The lack of a clear 'clear filter' button can cause users to assume the list is permanently empty, especially on small screens where the search bar might be out of focus.
**Action:** Always provide a clear, easily accessible 'Clear Search/Filter' CTA button directly within the empty state view to allow users to quickly reset their view and continue their journey.
